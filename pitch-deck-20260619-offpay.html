THE PROBLEM

Crypto wallets still behave like manual trading terminals.

Payments leak context

Addresses, balances, and payment trails are exposed by default.

Real-world checkout breaks

Bad connectivity should not stop stablecoin payment flows.

AI is stuck at advice

Agents can suggest actions but users still assemble every transaction.

The result: payments, payroll, swaps, and trading all happen in separate flows with no safe automation layer.

22 seconds
Keep this blunt. Wallets are good for holding and clicking, but real payment users need privacy, fallback when the network is weak, and help preparing complex actions. That is the opening for OffPay.
